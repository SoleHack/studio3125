Exceptional workspaces for extraordinary companies

Tiny, cramped offices aren’t conducive to inspiring creativity. If four blank walls and a single dry erase board are all you have to push your team to the next level, you’re simply not going to get there. Studio3125 is a bright, open, and inspiring place. Full of creators, marketers, cinematographers, animators, artists, and designers of all trades. So, what are you waiting for? Come see why we can help you take your next idea from good to exceptional.


A big space to bring you even bigger ideas

Studio3125 is a place to bring your big ideas to life. Need to film an entire commercial? Action! Need special effects and green screen help? Let’s get suited up. Need to record a voiceover or podcast? We’re all ears. Need a team of creative experts to help guide you from idea to final product? We’ve got the roadmap. Just need a space to meet with your team that isn’t just another boring hotel conference room? Welcome to Studio3125.

We offer so much more than just a creative space. We have the equipment, tools, know-how and most importantly, space, to get you across the finish line. Conveniently located just off I-10 and Broadway in Tempe, Arizona, Studio3125 is your new home away from home for meetings, shared workspace, creative professionals, video shoots, photo shoots, designers, coders, artists, and so much more. Take a look at our list of offerings below and sign up for a tour. We would love to show you in person just what we can do for you.
